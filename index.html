About Our Farm

Wandala Farms Ltd was established in August 2014 with RC. 1209207 as a mixed farming business. We take pride in sustainable farming practices and animal welfare, ensuring that all our products meet the highest standards of quality.

The farm also specializes in commodity trading, warehousing business as well as farm consulting services. It combines traditional methods with modern sustainable farming practices to deliver the best quality products to its customers.

Our Products & Services

Livestock

Healthy cattle, goats and sheep for meat and milk production as well as poultry products. These animals are raised with care and balanced nutrition that meet individual and commercial needs.

Grains

Premium quality rice, maize, sorghum, cowpeas, soybeans and cotton. Our grains are produced using organic practices and harvested at peak freshness.

Supply Services

Reliable delivery of farm products to businesses, restaurants, and individuals. We ensure timely delivery while maintaining product quality.
