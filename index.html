About Our Farm

Wandala Farms has been a family owned agricultural business since 2017. We take pride in sustainable farming practices and animal welfare, ensuring that all our products meet the highest standards of quality.

Our farm specializes in livestock including cattle, goats, sheep, and poultry, as well as a variety of grains like wheat, rice, maize, and beans. We're committed to providing fresh, healthy products across Nigeria.

We combine traditional methods with modern sustainable practices to deliver the best farm products to our customers.

Our Products & Services

Livestock

Healthy cattle, goats, sheep, and poultry raised with care and proper nutrition. We provide quality livestock for both commercial and individual needs.

Grains

Premium quality wheat, rice, maize, and beans. Our grains are grown using sustainable practices and harvested at peak freshness.

Supply Services

Reliable delivery of farm products to businesses, restaurants, and individuals. We ensure timely delivery while maintaining product quality.

Farm Consultation

Expert advice on livestock management, crop cultivation, and sustainable farming practices for both new and experienced farmers.
